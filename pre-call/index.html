Final Step · Prepare

Show Up Ready

Do these 5 things before the call. It's the difference between a useful session and a wasted hour.

✓

Know Your Current Holdings

Have a rough breakdown of your portfolio: coins, amounts, and where they're held (wallet vs exchange).

✓

Decide Your Monthly Income Goal

Not a fantasy number. What would actually change your life? $500/month? $2,000? $5,000? We'll reverse-engineer from there.

✓

Know Your Time Commitment

How many hours per week can you realistically put into this? Be honest with yourself. We'll build a plan that fits your life.

✓

Write Down Your Top 3 Questions

Security concerns, strategy questions, doubts, anything. Get them out of your head and onto paper before the call.

✓

Whitelist Our Email

Add team@altcoinpro.com to your contacts so you get the Zoom link on time. Check spam if you haven't seen our confirmation.
